About me

What I do
I am a photographer working at the intersection of advertising and documentary practice, creating visually compelling narratives for brands and non-profits. My work is rooted in storytelling — capturing moments that are not only aesthetically engaging but also meaningful and human.
My Background
With a postgraduate background in Advertising and Documentary Photography, I bring over a decade of freelance experience since 2011. Over the years, I have collaborated across sectors, shaping visual stories that resonate with diverse audiences while staying true to the authenticity of each subject.
My Approach
My approach is guided by a deep responsiveness to evolving visual cultures. I see photography as both a craft and a conversation — one that adapts, questions, and connects. Alongside my practice, I have contributed to photography education by designing and teaching a course on Documentary and Fashion Photography at AAFT, Noida, and by setting up a photography lab and studio at IIAD, New Delhi.
Career
  • Core Team Curator — Delhi Photo Festival (2011, 2013, 2015)
  • Photography Educator — AAFT, Noida
  • Studio Setup Lead — IIAD, New Delhi
  • Freelance Photographer — Advertising & Non-Profits (2011 – Present)
